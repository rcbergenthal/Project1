Utah's Premier Clinic For Those Dealing with Guilt and Shame, Peaceful River was built to help residents of Utah and Idaho deal with religious OCD/ Scrupulosity, childhood trauma, and any other cause of inappropriate guilt and shame.

Meet the Therapist

Hi, I'm Grace Bithell, LCSW. I’m a Utah-based shame and guilt therapist, a mostly recovered guilt-a-holic, and I value my relationships deeply. I understand that trauma and OCD can take away your ability to feel peace. I know how exhausting it is when your own mind doesn’t feel like a safe place. I care about providing therapy that helps you feel better, not just function better. I'm rarely someone’s first therapist. When you've tried exposures, learned to deep breathe, adapted to thinking positively, and are still experiencing intense distress from scrupulosity or trauma, I can get you unstuck. If you struggle to feel in your bones that you are good enough, live in Utah or Idaho, and you’re ready to move past coping and towards healing, reach out to me.

Services

  • OCD & Scrupulosity: Exposure and Response Prevention therapy (ERP) is considered the gold standard for OCD treatment. This type of therapy will focus on identifying your compulsions and interrupting them to then weaken the obsession.
  • Trauma: I've received training in EMDR, internal family systems, and the developmental needs meeting strategy. I'm comfortable working with difficult situations, dissociative individuals, and complicated attachment need
  • EMDR Intensives: An EMDR intensive is a powerful treatment that yields fast results. They usually last about three hours and most people do one to three intensives.
  • ARFID: I use a mix of trauma and exposure therapies to help you reduce the fear and anxiety food brings. I will meet you where you are at and not push you to do anything you aren't ready for.
